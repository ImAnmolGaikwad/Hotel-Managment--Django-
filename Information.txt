User Management:
    Admin: Full control over user management, including CRUD operations for managers and guests.
    Manager: Capability to manage users (including themselves) and rooms within their assigned scope.
    User: Basic CRUD operations for managing their own profile and accessing room availability information.

Room Management:
        CRUD operations for managing room inventory, including room types, availability, and pricing.
    Ability to check room availability and make reservations based on guest preferences.

Authorization and Permissions:
    Admin: Authorized to perform CRUD operations on all entities, including managers, users, and rooms.
    Manager: Granted permissions to manage users (including themselves) and rooms within their designated area.
    User: Limited permissions to manage their own profile and access room availability information.
